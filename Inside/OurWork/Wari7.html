Majhi ST Gharoghari, Majhi ST Manomani

May 9, 2019

Special MSRTC merchandise developed by us (Ex: Miniature MSRTC models, Coffee Mug, Calendar, Key Chains). As MSRTC enthusiasta, we are always get attracted towards MSRTC buses and this affection led us to create many miniature MSRTC bus models. We have experimented Paper, Metal, Acrylic Sheets, and many other materials to create miniature MSRTC bus Models. These models look so attractive that they are the center of attraction at all of our exhibitions.


Whenever we would visit a fair, we use to find many types of miniature models of vehicles for sale, but sadly we never found any miniature models of MSRTC buses. As MSRTC enthusiasts, we needed miniature bus models, but never find it nearby us, so this gave us an idea to develop our own set of Miniature Bus models. These Miniature Bus models will also be made available for sale at the original price (Price at which we bought).


Apart from Miniature MSRTC bus models, we have also made many other things which one can keep in his collection or can be given as souvenirs. Till now, we have developed merchandise like Coffee Mug, Miniature MSRTC Bus Models, Key Chain, Badges, Calendar, Collection of Old tickets, Photo Frames, etc. After Indian Railways, MSRTC would be the first SRTC to use these souvenirs for their promotion and Bus For Us Foundation is helping MSRTC in regards to this.


The Cabinet Minister of Transport of Maharashtra Government and Chairman of MSRTC has took special interest in this and he has a big collection of all these MSRTC souvenirs. He also thanked us for developing all these miniature models and other articles.
